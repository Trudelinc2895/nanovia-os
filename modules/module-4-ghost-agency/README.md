## CONCEPT
You automate repetitive business tasks for clients — they pay, you deliver, they don't need to understand the tech.

## TARGET NICHES (ranked by easiness + revenue)
1. **Real estate agents** — lead follow-up, listing descriptions, appointment confirmations
2. **E-commerce (Shopify)** — abandoned cart messages, review requests, inventory alerts
3. **Local service businesses** — quote follow-up, appointment reminders, Google review requests
4. **Coaches/consultants** — onboarding automation, email sequences, content repurposing
